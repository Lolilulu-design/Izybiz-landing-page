This blog is written by one person — 30 years of operational leadership across tech, SaaS, retail, and telecoms. No editorial team, no content calendar optimized for engagement. Every article comes from a real situation: a mission delivered, a project that went sideways, a decision that had to be made under pressure.

If you're a CEO, CFO, or board member of a company between €10M and €200M revenue, you'll find analysis here that assumes you already know the basics.

## What this blog covers

### [Interim management](/interim-management)

When it's the right move, how it actually works, and why it's different from hiring a consulting firm or running a slow CDI recruitment process. The situations that call for an interim executive — and the ones that don't.

### [AI integration in the enterprise](/ai-integration)

Why 95% of enterprise AI projects fail — and why it's almost never a technology problem. Governance, use case selection, adoption management: what needs to be in place for AI to deliver measurable results.

### Digital transformation in West Africa

Super app launches, mobile money, social commerce, fintech, brand deployment. Articles from fieldwork in the region — not desk research. What works, what drifts, what most operators underestimate before they're in it.

### [Scale-up & operational industrialisation](/scale-up-operations)

Series B, process structuring, delivery at scale. What founders find out too late — and how to get ahead of it before growth becomes the problem.
